This user owns or is a custodian of raw data, pipelines, portal, tools and/or processed data. They are usually a DCC PI.

This user has a vested interest in making sure their data is available, widely used, and used appropriately. They are concerned both that their data is being used within the confines of its use policy, and also that users are making sound statistical and biological inferences with it. -   May require some administrative access to an interface
